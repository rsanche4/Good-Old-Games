Iva Somnia is a short horror game which relies on exploration for storytelling. 
There isn't a main storyline or much dialogue, so players may have different 
interpretations on its meaning. Players will have to explore the world in order 
to reach its ending.

Available for Windows and Mac. You can also play the browser version of the game.

https://raffys-playground.itch.io/iva-somnia
